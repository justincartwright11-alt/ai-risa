# Recommended Adjustments

1. Confidence calibration: Compress 50–69% buckets and expand 70+% bucket to better match observed hit rates and reduce overconfidence.
2. Stoppage propensity calibration: Lower the threshold for stoppage calls to reduce overprediction, especially in borderline cases.
3. Round-finish tendency remapping: Adjust round-finish mapping to reduce early/late finish variance and improve round accuracy.
4. Decision/stoppage threshold tuning: Fine-tune the method threshold to reduce confusion between decision and stoppage outcomes.
