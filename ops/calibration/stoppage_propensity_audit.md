# Stoppage Propensity Audit

## Stoppage Propensity Bias:
- Stoppage predicted: 7/15 (47%)
- Stoppage actual: 6/15 (40%)
- Net bias: +7%

## Round-Finish Tendency Error Patterns:
- Most round errors occur in early stoppages (predicted late, actual early)
- No systematic late/early finish bias, but high variance

## Key Findings:
- Model slightly overpredicts stoppage overall
- Round-finish errors are not strongly directional but are frequent
